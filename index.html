Portfolio 作品集

Quant & Risk Portfolio 量化与风险项目集

I am interested in the point where financial models meet real portfolio decisions: how assumptions are validated, how risk is measured before it becomes loss, and how empirical signals can be tested without overclaiming. My current focus is model validation, investment risk, and quantitative investment research, especially projects that turn noisy market or customer data into disciplined, explainable analysis. 我感兴趣的是金融模型如何真正服务于投资决策：模型假设如何验证，风险如何在形成损失前被识别，实证信号如何在不过度解读的前提下被检验。当前我主要关注模型验证、投资风险和量化投资研究，尤其是如何把噪声很强的市场数据或客户数据转化为有纪律、可解释的分析。
