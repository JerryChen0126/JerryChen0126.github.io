Meme Volatility Spread Is Fragile Meme 驱动波动率作为定价风险因子

What mattered most was not just finding a return spread. I learned how a factor idea has to become a full test: clean intraday data, define the signal, estimate exposure before the test window, sort portfolios out of sample, and then stress the result with controls. The signal is interesting, but the short event window keeps it from being a trading claim. 重点和心得：这个项目最有价值的部分不是简单找 alpha，而是完整搭建了一条因子研究流程：从 1 分钟行情到实现波动率，再到 MDV beta 估计和样本外组合排序。我的主要收获是，一个看起来合理的风险因子，一旦加入市场控制、CPCV 和小样本稳健性检验，就会变得更难被严谨地证明。

Research Question研究问题

I wanted to separate a media story from a risk-pricing story. The media story is that names such as BBBY, GME, AMC, KOSS, and BB moved violently during retail trading episodes. The risk-pricing question is narrower: if I construct a volatility factor from those names, do other stocks with higher exposure to that factor subsequently earn different returns? 我想把“媒体事件”和“风险定价问题”分开。媒体层面是 BBBY、GME、AMC、KOSS、BB 等股票在散户交易事件中剧烈波动；真正的定量问题是：如果用这些股票构造一个波动率因子，其他股票对该因子的暴露是否能解释之后的收益差异。

\[ R_{i,t}=\alpha_i+\beta_{i,\mathrm{MDV}}\Delta \mathrm{MDV}_t+\varepsilon_{i,t} \]

In plain language, beta is the stock's sensitivity to the meme-volatility shock. A high beta means the stock tends to move more when the meme-volatility factor jumps. I estimate that sensitivity first, then use it to form portfolios later. 核心变量是 beta_i,MDV。我先在样本前半段估计每只股票的 MDV 暴露，再按暴露排序，在样本后半段做样本外组合检验。

Factor Construction因子构造

For each meme stock, I compute 1-minute log returns and aggregate squared intraday returns into daily realized volatility. The MDV factor is the equal-weighted average of realized volatility across the five meme names. The regression uses the daily change in this factor. 对每只 meme 股票，我先计算 1 分钟对数收益，再把日内平方收益加总成日度实现波动率。MDV 因子是五只 meme 股票实现波动率的等权平均，回归中使用该因子的日度变化。

\[ RV_{i,d}=\sqrt{\sum_{m=1}^{M} r_{i,m,d}^{2}},\quad \mathrm{MDV}_d=\frac{1}{5}\sum_{j\in\mathcal{M}} RV_{j,d} \]

I also built a RiskLabAI version with dollar bars and fixed-width fractional differentiation. The dollar bar thresholds scale with average daily dollar volume, so a liquid stock such as AMC uses a much larger threshold than KOSS. 我还做了 RiskLabAI 版本：使用 dollar bars 和固定宽度分数差分。Dollar bar 的阈值随平均日成交金额调整，所以 AMC 这类高流动性股票的阈值明显高于 KOSS。

Portfolio Test组合检验

The sample is split at August 15, 2022. Betas are estimated from July 1 to August 15, then the stocks are sorted into five groups and evaluated from August 16 to September 30. The long-short portfolio is low-MDV-beta minus high-MDV-beta. 样本以 2022 年 8 月 15 日为分割点。7 月 1 日至 8 月 15 日用于估计 beta，8 月 16 日至 9 月 30 日用于样本外组合检验。多空组合为低 MDV beta 组减高 MDV beta 组。

split_date = 2022-08-15; group = qcut(beta_MDV, 5); Low-High = Group1 - Group5

The low-beta side lost less than the high-beta side during the out-of-sample window, producing a positive low-minus-high spread. The result is economically visible but statistically weak, so I read it as a directional signal rather than a tradable claim. 样本外窗口中，低 beta 组下跌幅度小于高 beta 组，因此低减高组合为正。这个结果有经济含义，但统计显著性偏弱，所以我把它理解为方向性信号，而不是可直接交易的结论。

Robustness and Limits稳健性与局限

  • Cross-sectional pricing regression gives gamma = -0.0043, t = -1.00, p = 0.325, R² = 0.04, and Spearman correlation = -0.27.横截面定价回归得到 gamma = -0.0043，t = -1.00，p = 0.325，R² = 0.04，Spearman 相关为 -0.27。
  • Adding market and VIX controls does not fully reshuffle exposures: beta-rank correlations are 0.90 for MDV + market and 0.86 for MDV + market + VIX.加入市场和 VIX 控制后，暴露排序没有完全重排：MDV+市场的 beta 排名相关为 0.90，MDV+市场+VIX 为 0.86。
  • The market-control low-minus-high mean is 0.75% with t = 1.40.加入市场控制后的低减高组合均值为 0.75%，t 值为 1.40。
  • A random forest volatility-decay classifier reaches 0.483 accuracy and 0.488 ROC AUC, which is close to noise and prevents overclaiming.随机森林波动衰减分类器准确率为 0.483，ROC AUC 为 0.488，接近噪声，因此不能过度解读。
  • Combinatorial purged CV reports a negative mean Sharpe and near-zero DSR probability, which is a useful warning about small-sample fragility.组合式 purged CV 得到负的平均 Sharpe 和接近 0 的 DSR 概率，说明小样本结果比较脆弱。
